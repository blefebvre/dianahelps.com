About

Welcome! I have over 10 years of experience counselling individuals and leading group therapy in a variety of contexts. My work as a Registered Psychotherapist is client centered, and incorporates physical and psychosocial education to help clients toward their goals. I have a PhD in Educational Counselling from the University of Ottawa, where I studied psychotherapy processes and researched eating disorder treatment.

Beyond counselling work, I try to laugh with my husband, grow with our children, like the idea of staying fit, read to better understand our world, and get cranky when I'm hungry or tired.

Practice

  • Disordered Eating, Body Image and Self Esteem
  • Anxiety and Depression
  • Burnout
  • Trauma
  • Communication and Relationships

I like helping individuals navigate personal and professional development. I seek to recognize the needs of students and support adults as they care for themselves and their loved ones. I have extensive experience in sport and working with athletes. Whatever the challenge, I use evidence-based treatments to understand the past, find purpose in the present, and encourage growth moving forward.

My clinical work is supervised by Dr. Barbara Virley, Registered Psychologist.
