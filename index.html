About

Diana works with individuals experiencing a variety of personal challenges, and provides understanding in difficult times. Her practice as a Certified Canadian Counselor is client centered, and incorporates physical and psychosocial education to help clients toward their goals. She is a member in the Academy for Eating Disorders and researches psychotherapy processes at the University of Ottawa.
Areas of practice include:
  • Disordered Eating, Body Image and Self Esteem
  • Anxiety and Depression
  • Burnout
  • Trauma
  • Communication and Relationships
  • Identity and Motivational Development

Clients

Diana helps individuals of all ages negotiate personal and professional development. She recognizes the needs of students and young adults, and supports parents as they care for themselves and their loved ones. Diana has extensive experience in sport and can assist individuals and teams deal with the realities, pressures, and transitions incumbent with competition.

Services

  • Individual Psychotherapy
  • Group Counseling
  • Speaking and Educational Presentations
  • Nutritional Counseling & Meal Support with associate Registered Dietitians

Diana's clinical work is supervised by Dr. Barbara Virley, Registered Psychologist.
